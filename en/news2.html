Pottery Workshops · News 1 November 2025

New workshop: Wheel Throwing.

We're thrilled to introduce our brand new Wheel Throwing Workshop at Puracerâmica! Discover the art of shaping clay on the pottery wheel and create your own unique ceramic pieces, guided step by step by our experienced instructors.

This hands-on class is perfect for beginners and anyone curious about trying the potter's wheel for the first time. Bring a friend or partner and enjoy a creative session together in our studio.

Price €120 for two people — all materials included
Duration 2 hours
Location PURACERÂMICA Studio, Rua Jau 23a, Lisbon
