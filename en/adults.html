Birthdays & Celebrations

Your celebration, handmade.

Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any excuse to gather your favourite people.

What's included

  • Choose from our selection of ceramic pieces: mugs, bowls, plates, and more
  • All painting materials and glaze
  • Guidance from our team throughout your visit
  • Firing and professional glazing

Good to know

  • Suitable for all ages and skill levels
  • Bring your own cake or snacks, just let us know in advance
  • Ready for pickup in 7–14 days
Bachelorette Parties

A creative twist for the bride-to-be.

Paint personalized pieces together: mugs for your morning coffee, plates for your new home, or a vase for the many flower bouquets to come.

Make it special

  • Add a welcome drink for your group (€5 per person, request when booking)
  • Bring decorations or snacks to personalize your celebration
  • Create matching pieces or coordinate designs with our guidance

Perfect as a morning activity before the evening festivities, or a relaxed afternoon together.

Keepsakes

The smallest prints, kept forever.

Capture your baby's tiny hands or feet on ceramic tiles or plates. These precious prints become family heirlooms you'll treasure forever.

Safe for everyone

  • All materials are 100% non-toxic and safe for pregnant women and babies

How it works

  • We recommend two adults: one holds the baby while the other helps with the print
  • Add names, dates, or a short message once the print is done

At home option

  • Prefer to do this at home? Our Paint To Go kit includes everything you need
Before you come

Practical information.

Duration

Sessions last around 2 hours. Open Wednesday to Sunday, morning and afternoon slots available.

Pricing

Pay only for the pieces you choose. All materials, firing, and glazing included. No hidden fees.

Booking

Groups up to 6 can book directly online. Groups from 7–12 will be asked for a deposit, deducted on the day.

Pickup

Your finished piece is ready in 7–14 days. Come by during our pickup hours.
