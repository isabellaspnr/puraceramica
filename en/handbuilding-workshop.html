About this workshop

Discover the joy of handbuilding ceramics in this creative workshop. We'll explore fundamental techniques such as pinching, coiling, and slab-building. No prior experience is required. Whether you come with a clear idea of what you'd like to create or simply a curiosity to try something new, everyone is welcome.

After shaping your pottery, you'll also have the chance to paint and decorate your piece, adding your own personal touch.

What you'll learn

—
Pinching

The most direct technique: squeeze and shape a ball of clay with your fingers to form bowls, cups, and organic forms.

—
Coiling

Roll clay into long ropes and build up walls layer by layer. Great for larger, more sculptural pieces.

—
Slab building

Roll flat sheets of clay and fold, cut, and join them into forms. Perfect for plates, tiles, and angular shapes.

—
Decoration & painting

Add texture, stamps, and painted details to personalise your piece before it goes into the kiln.
