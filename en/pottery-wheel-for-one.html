About this session

A private pottery wheel session is the most personal way to discover one of the oldest crafts in the world. You'll have the wheel entirely to yourself for two full hours.

—
Centering

The foundation of wheel throwing. Learning to center a lump of clay on the spinning wheel, which takes focus, technique, and a little (or a lot of) patience.

—
Opening & pulling

Once centered, you'll open the clay and pull up the walls to form a cylinder. The starting point for most wheel-thrown shapes.

—
Shaping

Gently push and guide the walls to create your desired form. A bowl, a cup, a small vase.
