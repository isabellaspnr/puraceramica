About this workshop

Experience the art of wheel throwing in your own private session. This workshop is designed for individual learners who want focused, personal instruction — you'll have the potter's wheel all to yourself and receive the instructor's full, undivided attention throughout 2 hours.

Whether you're treating yourself to a creative experience, working on a specific skill, or looking for a unique personal gift idea — this is the most immersive way to discover wheel throwing.

What you'll learn

—
Centering

The most challenging part of wheel throwing — learning to center a lump of clay on the spinning wheel with focus and technique.

—
Opening & pulling

Open the centered clay and pull up the walls to form a cylinder — the starting point for most wheel-thrown shapes.

—
Shaping

Gently guide the walls to create your desired form — a bowl, a cup, a small vase. Each piece is entirely your own.
