Book our studio exclusively for your private celebration

Looking to celebrate something special? PURACERÂMICA offers the perfect creative space for your private event. Whether it's a birthday party, hen party, baby shower, or a unique gathering with friends and family — make our studio your own for a few hours.

Creative experience

Paint your own ceramics in a relaxed, inspiring atmosphere with all materials provided.

Up to 60 guests

Exclusive use of our entire two-floor studio space. The whole place is yours.

Bring your own

Bring food, drinks, cake, and decorations to personalise your celebration.

Flexible timing

Choose a time that works for you, including evenings and weekends.

Special occasions

Perfect for birthdays, team building, hen parties, baby showers, and anniversaries.

Memorable keepsakes

Everyone takes home their own hand-painted, professionally fired ceramic piece.

What's included

  • Exclusive studio access — the entire space is yours for the duration
  • All ceramic materials — mugs, plates, bowls and decorative pieces
  • Paints and tools — professional ceramic paint, brushes, stencils and sponges
  • Expert guidance — our team is with you throughout
  • Glazing and firing — all pieces professionally finished
  • Tables and seating — comfortable setup for your group

What you can bring

  • Food and beverages (including alcoholic drinks)
  • Birthday cake or desserts
  • Decorations to personalise the space
  • Music playlist — speakers available

Good to know

Pickup: Finished ceramics are ready approximately 7–14 days after your event.
Duration: Most events run 2–3 hours, but we're flexible.
