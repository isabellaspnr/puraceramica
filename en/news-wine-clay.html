Wine & Paint Evenings.

Join us for a new weekly pottery painting experience. Every Thursday from 18:00 to 20:00, come along for a relaxed session of pottery painting paired with a glass of wine or a non-alcoholic drink. Whether you come with friends or on your own, it's the perfect way to unwind after a long day and enjoy a creative, social evening.

When?

Thursdays, 18:00 – 20:00

Price of your ceramic + 6€ per person / includes 2 glasses of wine or non-alcoholic drink
