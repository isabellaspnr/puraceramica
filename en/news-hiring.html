We're hiring.

We are currently looking for a General Studio Assistant to join our team at PURACERÂMICA. This is a hands-on role at the heart of our studio: supporting day-to-day operations, looking after our customers, and helping to keep the space running smoothly.

If you love ceramics, care about craft and enjoy working with people, we would love to hear from you.

The Role

As a General Studio Assistant, your responsibilities will include:

  • Welcoming and attending to customers during studio sessions
  • Assisting with glazing and general clay work
  • Maintaining the cleanliness and organisation of the studio
  • Supporting our team during workshops and events
  • General studio upkeep and preparation

What We Look For

We are looking for someone reliable, hands-on and enthusiastic about working in a creative environment. Prior experience with ceramics or pottery painting is a plus, but not required. A willingness to learn, a good attitude and a genuine interest in craft are what matter most to us.

How to Apply

Please send us your CV along with a short introduction about yourself: who you are, what you do, and why you feel PURACERÂMICA is the right fit for you. We look forward to hearing from you.
