About this course

Do you want to learn the basics of handbuilding out of clay? Our 4-week pottery course is a great opportunity for anyone who wants to slow down, learn something new or refresh their skills. In this intensive course you will learn the essential techniques that will give you the foundation to create out of clay: pinching, slab-building and coiling. At the end, you will glaze or paint your pieces. Your teacher will guide you through the process while encouraging you to explore and experiment.

What to expect, week by week

01
Pinching

Introduction to ceramic processes and the pinch technique.

02
Coiling

Introduction to the coiling technique. The ideal method for building larger objects by stacking rolled ropes of clay.

03
Slab building

Learning the slab-building technique using flat sheets of clay to create plates, tiles and angular forms.

04
Glazing

Glaze all your pieces in clear glaze using different techniques: dipping, brushing or pouring. Different colors of underglaze for painting are available.

Throughout this course you'll also learn decorating techniques such as texturing and stamping, and how to join different clay parts such as adding handles to mugs.
