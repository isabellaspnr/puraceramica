Studio · News 3 March 2026

Membership soon.

We're so excited to share something we've been working on for a while: We are finally opening our doors for independent creators!
Very soon, you'll be able to become a member and use our studio as your own creative space.

Whether you're an experienced ceramic artist looking for a place to work, or someone who's just started their journey and wants to refine their skills, we are happy to have you.

What's included

Membership gives you access to the studio to work independently, with everything you need already there:

  • Access to pottery wheel
  • Kiln firing for your pieces
  • Studio tools and equipment
  • Clay for purchase

We hope to launch this April/May, in the meantime you can come by for a pottery workshop and get to know us and the studio.

Status Coming soon — stay tuned!

Sign up on our membership page to be the first to know when we launch.
