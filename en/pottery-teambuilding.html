Pottery Workshops · Groups & Teams

Build something together.

Are you looking for a creative and impactful team-building activity? Then our pottery team-building workshop is just right for you! Away from the everyday work routine, you'll create something with your hands, strengthen team spirit, and take home lasting memories.

What to expect

From clay to functional pottery.

All participants will work with real clay using different handbuilding techniques, depending on what they are wanting to create. You'll get a short introduction to the basics, then you can jump straight into making. Our instructors are there throughout the session — answering questions, demonstrating steps, and helping when you need it — while giving you the space to explore and create your piece yourself. Just hands, clay, and a couple of hours away from the screen.

Each person leaves with their own piece, which we glaze and fire in our kiln. Finished ceramics are ready for pickup about three to four weeks later.

Handbuilding techniques

Three ways to shape clay.

Coiling

Roll clay into long ropes and build up walls layer by layer. A meditative, intuitive technique perfect for bowls and vases.

Slab building

Roll out flat sheets of clay and fold, cut, and join them into forms. Great for plates, tiles, and angular shapes.

Pinching

The most direct technique: pinch and squeeze a ball of clay into shape with your fingers. The fastest way to get a feeling for clay.

We'll suggest the right technique based on what you'd like to create.
