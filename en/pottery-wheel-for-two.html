About this workshop

This workshop is designed for 2 people to share a unique creative experience. You'll each have your own wheel, your own clay, and the same patient instructor guiding you both through the fundamentals.

Perfect for couples, friends, or anyone who wants to share a creative experience. No pottery skills required, just good company.

What you'll learn

—
Centering

The foundation of wheel throwing. Learning to center a lump of clay on the spinning wheel, which takes focus, technique, and a little (or a lot of) patience.

—
Opening & pulling

Once centered, you'll open the clay and pull up the walls to form a cylinder. The starting point for most wheel-thrown shapes.

—
Shaping

Gently push and guide the walls to create your desired form. A bowl, a cup, a small vase.

What's included

— 2 pottery wheels (one each)
— All clay and materials for both
— Professional instructor guidance
— Glazing and firing of 2 of your pieces
