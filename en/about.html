Our Story · Since 2022

The PURACERÂMICA studio.

Since 2022, we've been Lisbon's home for pottery painting, a creative space where you can pick a ceramic piece and paint it your way. What started as a paint-your-own-pottery studio has grown into a welcoming community that now also offers traditional pottery classes and workshops.

Founded 2022 Alcântara, Lisbon All skill levels welcome
The beginnings

Welcome!

At PURACERÂMICA we set out to build a creative space for everbody. In this fast-paced individualistic world we want to create community and strengthen bonds over artistic expression and learn from eachother. With this, the idea of pottery painting on bisque-ware was born: intuitive for everyone without having to learn pottery first. Everyone takes home uniquely personalized ceramic, creating meaningful keepsakes that last.

We finally opened our doors in early 2022 due to delays caused by the pandemic. Though we were sure that pottery painting would bring creative minds together we did not anticipate the huge interest in ceramics and the creative process.

Through learning a lot in our first years running the studio and developing further professionally, we finally were ready to expand our studio. We now also offer pottery workshops for those who want to dive deeper into the craft.

Taking up something entirely new is intimidating, but where there is no risk there is no reward. That's why it's important for us to give everyone space to experiment in the comfortable environment we strive to create with our studio.

Come visit us and become part of our family!

Why we do this

Why pottery painting & ceramics?

Painting ceramics offers something our fast-paced world often lacks: the chance to slow down, focus on your brushstrokes, and watch colors come to life on ceramics. This process can be meditative, helping you to focus on what's right in front of you, often without you even realizing it.

Pottery is one of the world's oldest crafts, with many pieces surviving millenia. We want people to be able to create something they will cherish for years, knowing they made it with a vision.

Join us for open studio time to paint your own pottery, explore our workshops to learn new techniques, or dive into traditional pottery on our Clayground. No experience needed - Just bring curiosity and a willingness to try.

What we stand for

Our values

Inclusivity

We want everyone to feel comfortable and at home in our studio. We make art accessible and take away the fear of "not being good at art". All skill levels and backgrounds are welcome.

Sustainability

We minimize waste through quality materials, plastic-free packaging, and biodegradable wrapping. Need a box to carry your pieces home? We've got recycled cardboard for that.

Growth

Everyone was a beginner once. We create an environment where you feel comfortable trying new things without fear of mistakes. We encourage experimentation and celebrate progress over perfection.

Community

Sharing knowledge and teaching others is integral to learning. Our studio's heart is you - our community, connected through art and the joy of making things by hand.
