Pottery Painting · Pet Special

A keepsake as unique as your pet.

Capture your furry friend's paw print on ceramic. A lasting memory you'll treasure forever. Safe, quick, and meaningful for dogs and cats of all sizes.

Dogs & cats welcome Takes just minutes From €22.50
Option 1

In-studio paw prints.

Bring your pet to our studio for a quick, stress-free experience. We'll capture their paw print on the ceramic piece of your choice.

How it works

  • Choose your ceramic piece: plates, mugs, tiles, or bowls
  • We use pet-safe, non-toxic, water-based colors
  • The whole process takes just a few minutes
  • Add your pet's name, a date, or a short message
  • We fire and glaze it for you
  • Ready for pickup in 7–14 days

Good to know

  • Price depends on your chosen ceramic piece
  • Your pet should be comfortable around other animals and people
  • Appointment recommended to ensure we have time for you

For nervous or anxious pets, we recommend our at-home Pet-Set instead.

Option 2

Create the paw print at home.

If your pet prefers the comfort of home — or gets anxious in new places — our Pet-Set lets you create their paw print keepsake at your own pace.

How it works

  • Visit our studio to choose your ceramic piece and up to 4 pet-safe colors
  • We prepare your Pet-Set with instructions, materials, and brushes
  • Create the paw print at home when your pet is relaxed
  • Return the finished piece to our studio
  • We fire and glaze it, ready for pickup in 7–14 days

Pricing

  • Large dog bowl: €35
  • Small dog bowl: €30
  • Cat bowl: €22.50
  • Plates, tiles, and other pieces: prices vary

Additional brushes and accessories available for purchase.

Before you come

Practical information.

Pet safety

All materials are 100% non-toxic and pet-safe. We use water-based colors that wash off easily. The process is quick and stress-free.

Perfect for

Dogs and cats of all sizes. A beautiful memorial keepsake, a gift for pet lovers, or simply a way to celebrate your furry family member.

Booking

For in-studio paw prints, we recommend booking ahead to ensure we have time for you. Email info@puraceramica.pt or call us. Pet-Sets can be purchased during opening hours — calling ahead is recommended to confirm availability.

Timeline

In-studio prints take just a few minutes. Pet-Set at your own pace at home. All finished pieces ready for pickup in 7–14 days after drop-off.

Tips for the best result

  • Bring your pet when they're calm and relaxed — after a walk is ideal
  • For at-home Pet-Sets, best when your pet is tired or content
  • Have treats ready as rewards
  • Clean paws beforehand for clearer prints
