Pottery Painting · Specials

A keepsake as unique as your pet.

Their paw print, permanently fired into ceramic. Come to the studio or create it at home with our take-home kit — we do the rest.

All pet sizes From €15 + €3 kit fee Firing included

Two ways to create it.

Whether your pet is a studio socialite or happiest at home, we have an option that works.

Option 1

In-studio paw prints.

Bring your pet to the studio. Our team guides you through the process — we press the paw, you choose colours and personalise the piece. Relaxed and quick.

Dogs & cats welcome · book in advance · note in observations
Option 2

Create the paw print at home.

Take the kit home, press the paw in your own space at your own pace, then bring the piece back to us for firing and glazing. Perfect for anxious pets.

Kit fee €3 · return within reasonable time
Before you come

Everything you need to know.

Pricing Large dog bowl €35 · Small dog bowl €30 · Cat bowl €22.50. All pieces include firing and glazing. Take-home kit + €3.
Booking Book online and note "paw print" in observations — or email info@puraceramica.pt with your preferred date.
Duration & pickup In-studio: around 1 hour. Take-home: paint at your pace, return when ready. Finished pieces ready in 7–14 days.
Good to know Pieces are food-safe after firing. We work with all pet sizes — if unsure, email us first.
