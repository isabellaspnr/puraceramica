What is the Open Studio?

In our studio you will find all tools and necessary equipment as well as glazes and clay. Basic knowledge is assumed here - the Open Studio is unattended.

We offer two different options to use the Open Studio:
Flex Hours or our Co-Create Membership.

Feel free to drop by during opening hours and our team will be happy to show you the studio. If you want, you can start right away!

Good to Know

Do you also sell clay?
▼

Yes! You can purchase clay directly in our studio. Prices vary depending on your membership level:

• Flex Hours: 12€ / 10.5kg
• Stamp Cards: 11€ / 10.5kg (10% discount)
• Co-Create Members: FREE monthly allocation included in membership (10-20kg depending on tier)

How does firing work?
▼

We fire your pieces in-house with our professional kilns. The process takes 7-14 days depending on studio capacity. You'll receive a notification via WhatsApp or email when your pieces are ready for pickup.

Firing costs: 6€/kg for Flex Hours, 5.50€/kg for Stamp Cards, and 4-5€/kg for Co-Create Members (with discounts up to 35% depending on tier).

What skills do I need to visit the Open Studio?
▼

Basic ceramics knowledge is assumed - the Open Studio is unattended. We recommend completing at least one of our workshops (e.g., Wheel Throwing or Hand-Building) before using the Open Studio independently.

If you're completely new to pottery, check out our beginner workshops first!
