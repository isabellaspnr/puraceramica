Pottery Painting · Corporate

Teambuilding, but make it creative.

Ceramic painting workshops that bring teams together. At our studio or your office. A break from the usual that actually brings people closer.

Up to 30 people 2–3 hours At our studio or yours
Option 1

Team workshop at our studio.

Bring your team to our studio in Alcântara for a creative break that sparks conversation and strengthens collaboration.

What's included

  • Guided ceramic painting session for up to 30 people
  • All materials, firing, and glazing
  • Each participant creates a piece to take home
  • Professional guidance from our team, no experience needed
  • Finished pieces ready for pickup in 7–14 days

Good to know

  • Duration: 2–3 hours depending on group size
  • Bookings available outside regular opening hours
  • Optional catering or welcome drinks available on request
  • Pricing depends on the ceramic piece chosen. Contact us for a quote
Option 2

We come to you.

We bring everything to your office: materials, setup, and guidance. Perfect for staff celebrations, team days, or client events without leaving your workplace.

How it works

  • We arrive with ceramic pieces and all supplies
  • Our team sets up and guides the entire session
  • We collect pieces after the event for firing and glazing
  • Finished pieces delivered back to your office in 7–21 days

Requirements

  • Tables and chairs for all participants
  • Access to water for cleaning brushes
  • Travel fee applies depending on distance from Alcântara
Why it works

Why ceramic teambuilding?

Breaks the routine

Step away from screens and meetings. This hands-on activity helps teams relax, recharge, and connect with each other in a new way.

Everyone participates

No artistic experience needed. Ceramic painting is accessible to everyone, perfect for diverse teams with different backgrounds and skill sets.

Something to take home

Participants leave with a mug, plate, or bowl they made themselves. A daily reminder of the experience and the people they shared it with.

Flexible

At our studio or yours, during or outside business hours. We work around your schedule and can accommodate groups of various sizes.

Planning your event

  • Book at least 3–4 weeks in advance for best availability — custom orders may require more lead time
  • We'll help you choose ceramic pieces that fit your budget
  • Evening and weekend sessions available
  • Optional add-ons: catering, welcome drinks, customized pieces
