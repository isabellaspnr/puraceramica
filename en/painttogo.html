Pottery Painting · Take Home

Paint To Go.

Take ceramics home to paint at your own pace. Perfect as a thoughtful gift or a relaxing activity for yourself. We provide everything you need and fire your finished piece.

All ages From €15 + kit fee Paint at your pace

How It Works

01

Visit the Studio

Come by to browse and choose your ceramic piece: mugs, plates, bowls, figurines, and more.

02

Pick Your Colors

Select up to 4 water-based, non-toxic paint colors per piece. Our team will help you choose.

03

Take Home Your Kit

We'll prepare your Paint To Go kit with instructions, 2 brushes, and your chosen colors. Additional brushes available for purchase.

04

Paint at Home

Create comfortably at your own pace. Follow the instructions provided or let your creativity flow.

05

Return for Firing

Bring your finished piece back to the studio. We'll glaze and fire it to make it food-safe and durable.

06

Pick Up or Ship

Collect your finished piece in 10–14 days, or we ship it to you (shipping fee applies).

Perfect For

  • Baby keepsakes done in the comfort of your home
  • Pet paw prints for a lasting memory of your furry friend
  • Quiet evenings when you want to relax and create without leaving your home
  • Group activities at home, perfect for small gatherings or family projects

Great for all ages and skill levels. Kids under 12 should have adult supervision.

Practical Information

What's Included

Paint To Go instruction kit, 2 brushes (additional available for purchase), up to 4 non-toxic water-based paint colors per piece, and professional glazing and firing.

Pricing

Price varies based on your chosen ceramic piece, starting from €15, plus a small kit preparation fee. Shipping available for an additional fee if you can't return to pick up.

Timeline

Once you return the piece we will glaze it with transparent glaze and fire it in the kiln. It will be ready for pickup 10–14 days after drop-off.

Studio Visits

Visit us during opening hours to choose your piece and collect your kit. Call ahead or write us a message to confirm availability of specific pieces.

Tips for painting at home

  • Cover your work surface with newspaper or a plastic sheet
  • Water-based paints wash off easily, but wear clothes you don't mind getting dirty
  • The colors will look different after firing. We'll show you color samples in the studio
  • Apply 2–3 coats rather than one thick coat for best results
