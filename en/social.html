Pottery Painting · Social Institutions

Creative moments for everyone.

Art connects people across all ages and abilities. We offer care homes, retirement centers, and social institutions for children and adults alike the opportunity to paint ceramic together. At our studio or at your facility.

2–3 hours Custom group pricing In-studio or on-site
Why it works

More than just creativity.

Ceramic painting is an activity that can feel therapeutic. It engages the mind, hands, and heart. The gentle, repetitive motions can be deeply calming, while the creative process encourages self-expression and connection.

Benefits include

  • Improved focus and hand-eye coordination
  • Sensory stimulation through colors, textures, and materials
  • Social interaction in a relaxed, non-pressured environment
  • A tangible keepsake that participants made themselves
  • Suitable for all skill levels and abilities

Each participant paints their own piece, guided with patience and care by our experienced team.

The process

How it works.

01

Get in touch

Contact us to discuss your group's needs, size, and any special requirements. We'll help you choose between in-studio or on-site sessions.

02

We prepare everything

We bring all materials, tools, and guidance. Choose from mugs, plates, bowls, or tiles — whatever suits your group best.

03

Paint together

Everyone paints at their own pace with support from our team. No pressure, just creativity and connection.

04

We fire & deliver

We take the pieces back for professional firing and glazing. In 7–21 days, we deliver the finished ceramics to your institution.

Before you book

Practical information.

Location

Sessions at our studio in Alcântara, or we bring everything to your facility. We adapt to what works best for your group.

Group size

From small groups of 5 up to 30 participants. We adapt the session to your group's size, pace, and specific needs.

Duration

Sessions typically last 2–3 hours, but we're flexible. We adjust timing to match your participants' energy levels and schedules.

Pricing

Special institutional pricing to ensure accessibility. Contact us for a custom quote based on group size and location.

What's included

  • All ceramic pieces and painting materials
  • Experienced facilitators who work with diverse groups
  • Patient, individualized guidance throughout the session
  • Professional firing and glazing of all pieces
  • Delivery of finished ceramics to your institution
  • 100% non-toxic, safe materials for all participants
