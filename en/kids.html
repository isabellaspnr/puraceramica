Pottery Painting · Kids

Creative fun for kids.

A space for kids to explore their creativity. Whether it's a birthday party, family day, or after-school activity: we're here to inspire.

Ages 4+ From €15 / child All materials included
Birthday Parties

A birthday they'll remember.

Let the kids create unforgettable memories together while making their own ceramic masterpieces to take home.

What's included

  • Each child chooses their own ceramic piece to paint (mugs, plates, figurines, and more)
  • All painting materials, brushes, and glazes
  • Guidance and support from our team
  • Firing and glazing, ready for pickup in 7–14 days

Good to know

  • Ages 6+ (younger children welcome with adult supervision)
  • Bring your own birthday cake, just let us know in advance
  • Duration: around 2 hours depending on group size and age
  • At least one adult must stay with children under 12

Planning a larger group? Email us at info@puraceramica.pt and we'll take care of the details.

Family Day

Create together, keep forever.

Spend quality time together as a family, creating pieces you'll use at home. Perfect for matching mugs, a coordinated dinnerware set, or just a relaxed afternoon together.

  • Ages 3+ welcome — our team adapts to every age group
  • Create individual pieces or work on a family project together
  • Our team helps younger children with painting techniques
  • Relaxed, pressure-free environment where everyone can take their time
After School & Friends

A creative break from the everyday.

A creative break from school stress. Kids can drop by with friends to paint, unwind, and create something they will be proud of.

  • Children under 12: one adult must supervise at all times
  • Duration: around 2 hours
  • Perfect for playdates, small friend groups, or solo creativity time

Parents can drop off older kids (12+) while the kids create.

Before you come

Practical information.

Age Requirements

Most activities are suitable for ages 6 and up. Younger children (3+) are welcome with individual adult supervision.

Pricing

Starting from €15 per child, based on the ceramic piece chosen. Includes all materials, firing, and glazing. No setup fees or hidden costs.

Booking

Book ahead for birthday parties and larger groups. Just email us with your preferred date and group size — we'll take care of the rest.

Duration & Pickup

Sessions typically last around 2 hours, though younger children may finish earlier. Finished pieces are ready for pickup in 7–14 days.

What to expect

  • A relaxed, creative atmosphere where mistakes are part of the fun
  • Ceramic pieces in fun shapes kids actually get excited about
  • Patient guidance from our team — we're happy to help!
  • Kids leave proud of what they've made
